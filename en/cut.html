High-Precision Laser Cutting

We provide high-precision laser cutting services using state-of-the-art CNC laser systems. Whether for thin sheets or heavy-duty metal, our equipment ensures clean, burr-free cuts with tight tolerances.

  • ⦿ Materials: Steel, Stainless Steel, Aluminum
  • ⦿ Accuracy: ±0.1 mm
  • ⦿ CAD/CAM integration for complex shapes
  • ⦿ High repeatability for large series
