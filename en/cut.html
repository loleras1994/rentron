Laser & CNC Punching for Sheet Metal

At Rentron SA, we offer a complete range of sheet metal cutting technologies — combining fiber laser and CNC turret punching to cover all geometries, volumes, and material requirements. Whether you need fast prototyping, perforated patterns, or burr-free cuts with tight tolerances, our advanced machinery delivers precise, repeatable results for every project.

  • Laser cutting (Bodor) — clean, precise cuts up to 20 mm
  • Turret punching (Amada Arcade, Vipros, AE) — fast and versatile for complex forms
  • Supports steel, stainless steel, and aluminum sheets
  • Accuracy: ±0.1 mm | Max sheet size: 3000 x 1500 mm
  • CAD/CAM file compatibility (DXF, DWG, STEP)

Applications

  • High-speed repetitive cuts using turret punching (Amada Arcade, Vipros, AE)
  • Forming louvers, countersinks, dimples, and embossing through punching
  • Perforated patterns and grid holes with tool-based consistency
  • Complex contours, irregular shapes, and smooth edges with laser cutting
  • Cutting thick or delicate materials with minimal heat impact (Bodor laser)

Why Choose Rentron

  • Three Amada punching machines for high throughput
  • Bodor fiber laser for tight tolerances and minimal finishing
  • Skilled operators and automated nesting for material efficiency
  • In-house quality control for consistent results
  • Flexible order sizes and short lead times
  • Seamless integration with other production steps
