About Rentron

Over four decades of excellence in precision metal constructions.

Rentron S.A.

Founded in 1979, Rentron S.A. is one of Greece’s largest industrial subcontracting companies, specializing in the design and manufacture of high-precision metal parts and custom sheet metal solutions.

Located in privately owned facilities in the industrial district of Isthmos Korinthos and with head offices in Athens, Rentron operates over 2,500m² of production space on a 15,000m² property.

Core Capabilities

  • R&D with advanced CAD/CAM software
  • Sheet metal processing: CNC punching, press brakes, shearing
  • High-quality welding (TIG, MIG-MAG, spot welding)
  • Powder coating & silk screen printing
  • Assembly & packing

Industries & Products

We serve diverse industries including telecom, electronics, solar, defense, and art. Our product lines include:

  • Heavy-duty industrial products
  • Display stands
  • Telecom cabinets and 19” racks
  • Custom enclosures and digital system structures
  • Solar system components

Certifications & Quality

Rentron is ISO 9001:2008 certified and utilizes a robust MRP II system for production control. Our staff includes qualified engineers and certified welding professionals to ensure top-tier quality.
