At Rentron SA, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to analyze traffic using Google Analytics. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You may control your preferences at any time.

Cookies Used
