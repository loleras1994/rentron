About Rentron

Precision. Innovation. Reliability.

“Trusted by global leaders, powered by precision and Greek craftsmanship.”

Rentron S.A. was founded in 1979 and is today one of the largest Greek companies specializing in the design and manufacture of high-precision metal parts. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has allowed us to thrive in a variety of demanding sectors.

Core Capabilities

  • R&D using CAD/CAM and ERP systems
  • CNC punching, laser cutting, bending
  • High-quality welding (TIG, MIG, spot)
  • Powder coating & silk screen printing
  • Full mechanical and electronic assembly

Industries & Products

  • Telecom cabinets & 19” racks
  • Display stands and kiosks
  • Photovoltaic & energy system parts
  • Custom metal enclosures
  • Smart city infrastructure
