RENTRON SA

Precision. Innovation. Reliability.

“Trusted by global leaders, powered by precision and Greek craftsmanship.”

Founded in 1979, Rentron S.A. is one of the leading industrial subcontracting companies in Greece, specializing in the design and construction of high-precision sheet metal components. With over four decades of experience, we combine innovative design, cutting-edge production technologies, and deep industry know-how to deliver reliable solutions for demanding markets worldwide.

Our operations span a privately owned 15,000m² industrial site in Korinthos with 2,500m² of modern facilities, supported by head offices in Athens. From telecom infrastructure to smart city solutions, our custom and standardized metal enclosures serve both Greek and international clients.

Core Capabilities

  • In-house R&D using CAD/CAM and MRP II systems
  • CNC punching, bending, laser cutting, shearing
  • Precision welding (TIG, MIG, spot)
  • Electrostatic powder coating & silk screen printing
  • Mechanical assembly and packaging
