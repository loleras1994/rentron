Industrial Powder Coating for Metal Parts

Our powder coating line delivers consistent, durable finishes with excellent adhesion, corrosion resistance, and visual appeal. Electrostatic application ensures even coverage, followed by baking in a controlled curing oven for optimal durability.

We offer full in-house finishing — from surface prep to coating — for steel, stainless steel, and aluminum parts. Choose from a wide range of RAL colors and textures (matte, fine structure, semi-gloss, gloss).

  • Max part size: 2000 x 1500 x 600 mm
  • Support for steel, stainless steel, and aluminum
  • RAL color library with matte, gloss, and texture options
  • Anti-corrosion & impact-resistant finish
  • Batch and one-off production accepted

Applications

  • Coated IT rack parts and enclosures
  • Industrial and electrical cabinets
  • Laser-cut brackets, flanges, or covers
  • Parts with pre-assembled PEM inserts
  • Final finish before packaging and shipping
