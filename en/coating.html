Powder Coating

Durable and professional finishes for your metal enclosures and parts.

High-Quality Powder Coating

Rentron S.A. offers premium powder coating services for maximum durability and aesthetics. We apply electrostatic powder to metal surfaces and bake them in controlled ovens, resulting in uniform, long-lasting finishes.

Our coating line supports a variety of colors, textures (matte, gloss, fine structure), and RAL codes to meet your specific project needs.

Applications include: IT enclosures, racks, industrial panels, and custom cabinets. We guarantee corrosion resistance, impact strength, and a professional look.
