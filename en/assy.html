Mechanical & Electromechanical Assembly

Rentron SA offers final assembly services for metal enclosures, racks, and technical products. We integrate mechanical and electrical components — from fasteners and hinges to submodules, wiring and labeling — so your product is fully tested and ready for deployment.

Our team can follow BOMs, wiring diagrams, or custom build instructions to deliver turnkey results.

  • Mechanical assembly with fasteners, studs, gaskets, and accessories
  • Mounting of PCBs, DIN rails, fans, or control devices
  • Basic wiring and cable routing upon request
  • Labeling, serial tagging, and quality inspection
  • Packaging and dispatch-ready preparation

Applications

  • Racks and IT systems with mounted accessories
  • Custom industrial boxes and control panels
  • OEM product subassemblies
  • Pre-wired enclosures with cable management
  • Final-step integration before shipping
