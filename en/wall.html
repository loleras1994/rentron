Wall Mounting Racks

Compact and functional racks for efficient wall-mounted installations.

Overview

The Rentron CW32 and CW35 series wall-mounted racks are ideal for small spaces such as offices and homes. They combine ergonomic design, elegant finish, and ease of installation while offering full access to equipment and efficient cable management.

CW32 Features

  • Adjustable mounting rails in depth
  • Supports up to 4 mounting rails
  • Front Plexiglass door with quick-release system
  • Security lock & 180° door opening
  • Side access panel with lock or screws
  • Top and bottom cable entries
  • Fan support on the top panel
  • IP20 / IK08 – Electrostatic RAL 7032 finish
  • Grounding point (M6)

CW35 Features

  • Dual-structure: fixed wall base & hinged section
  • Easy access to rear of equipment
  • Plexiglass door with reversible opening
  • Adjustable mounting rails in depth
  • Top and bottom ventilation slots
  • Front-section-only mounting option
  • Cable entries & grounding point
  • IP20 / IK08 – RAL 7032 powder-coated steel

Compliance & Build

  • Constructed from reinforced steel
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • Made in Greece
  • Compact design for easy wall-mounting
  • Ideal for telecom and network applications

CW32 Series

The CW32 series is ideal for small office or home installations. It offers a stylish design, easy access from the front and side, and optional fan integration for improved airflow. Its sturdy build and 4-point mounting capability make it suitable for demanding uses.

CW35 Series
