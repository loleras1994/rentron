Custom Silk Screen Printing

Rentron S.A. offers silk screen printing solutions tailored to your branding and technical requirements. Whether it’s logos, labels, instructions, or markings, we can print directly on powder-coated or raw metal surfaces using durable, high-contrast inks.

Our printing process ensures long-lasting visibility, resistance to fading, and excellent adhesion to painted or anodized surfaces. Ideal for telecom equipment, control panels, racks, and any custom metal structure.

We collaborate with your design files or offer in-house design assistance for layout and positioning.

  • Compatible with powder-coated, anodized, or raw metal surfaces
  • Multi-color printing in high contrast inks
  • Logos, part numbers, safety icons, and interface graphics
  • Durable UV-resistant inks
  • Custom positioning based on your DXF or PDF layout

Applications

  • IT rack components with front-panel branding
  • Control panels with functional labels and symbols
  • Warning and instruction markings for enclosures
  • Short-run production and prototyping
  • Multi-step jobs: cut → bend → paint → print

Why Choose Rentron

  • Screen printing after powder coating, no outsourcing
  • Fast integration with other manufacturing stages
  • Precise positioning for repeat jobs and templates
  • Colorfast and smudge-resistant finish
  • In-house design adaptation if needed
