Overview

The CR-10 floor-standing racks by Rentron SA are designed for demanding telecom, electronic, and AV installations. They combine years of engineering experience with modular features to ensure fast installation and robust performance.

Key Features

  • Steel frame (1.5–2.0 mm) for maximum rigidity
  • 4 adjustable 19” rails, depth-adjustable in 20 mm steps
  • Fan tray slots with access from inside or outside
  • Mounting base for casters or leveling feet
  • Optional dust filters and grounding bolts

Panels & Doors

  • Removable side panels with snap-locks
  • Front door with Plexiglass and two-point swing handle lock
  • Perforated or full metal door options
  • Rear panel with spring pins and cable cutouts
  • 180° door opening, reversible left/right

Compliance & Build

  • Complies with IEC 297-2, DIN 41494, EN 60950, BS 5954
  • Powder-coated polyester paint (anthracite & RAL 7032) or Black
  • Height extension option (100 mm base)
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • Made in Greece
