Sheet Metal Bending

Precision bending for all types of sheet metal components.

CNC Bending with Multi-Axis Press Brakes

Rentron SA offers advanced CNC bending services using four Amada multi-axis press brakes designed for precise metal forming. We can bend a variety of sheet metal components — from simple flanges to complex enclosure shapes — with high accuracy and minimal deformation. Our experienced operators ensure consistent quality across all production batches.

  • Bending length up to 3000 mm
  • Material thickness: up to 8 mm (steel)
  • Four Amada CNC press brakes with real-time angle control
  • High repeatability and bending angle accuracy ±0.3°
  • Compatible with pre-cut laser or punched parts

Applications

  • Forming U, Z, L and complex folded geometries
  • Creating box enclosures, covers, and trays from flat blanks
  • High-precision folds on mounting flanges and stiffeners
  • Multi-step bends for server rack doors and panels
  • Bending stainless steel, aluminum, and pre-coated sheets
